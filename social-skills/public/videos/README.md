# Scenario Videos

Place short MP4 clips here — one per scenario. Videos should be:
- 3–5 seconds long, looping
- Showing the social situation (child acting it out or simple animation)
- Muted (no audio in video — TTS handles narration)
- Compressed: target < 1 MB per clip

| Filename        | Scenario                                           |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------------------|
| scenario-1.mp4  | Friend took your toy                               |
| scenario-2.mp4  | Nobody wants to play with me                       |
| scenario-3.mp4  | Want to join friends' game                         |
| scenario-4.mp4  | Friend is crying and sad                           |
| scenario-5.mp4  | Made a mistake and broke something                 |
| scenario-6.mp4  | Friend is pushing me                               |
| scenario-7.mp4  | Friendship threat ("I won't be your friend")       |
| scenario-8.mp4  | Someone laughing at me                             |
| scenario-9.mp4  | Asked to keep a scary secret                       |
| scenario-10.mp4 | Pressured to do something that feels wrong         |

**If a video file is missing**, the app automatically shows the emoji scene strip instead — no crash.
